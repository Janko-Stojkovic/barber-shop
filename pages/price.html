Our services

Haircuts & Styling

There’s no better time than the new year to embrace a new look. Continuing from last year’s exploration of bolder cuts and styles, this year is offering up some of the best men’s looks.

Beard Treatment

Beard treatments can be used on a daily basis, or weekly basis, depending on your needs and preference. Intense treatments are usually recommended for weekly basis routine.

Coloring Service

All color and highlighting charges are calculated at using 50 grams of product. Additional colors, long or thick hair may require more product use and therefore will increase the color charge.

Beard waxing

Shaving is a pain, most men agree. If your goal is to reduce how often you shave, then waxing is a great choice. Waxing removes facial hair at the root rather than simply cutting it.
